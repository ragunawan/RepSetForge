# RepSetForge Privacy Policy

Effective date: TODO before submission

RepSetForge is a strength-training tracker for iPhone. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

## Data RepSetForge Stores

RepSetForge stores the workout and training data you enter, including exercises, routines, sets, reps, weights, RPE values, body metrics, and notes. This data is used only to provide app features such as workout logging, history, progress charts, progression prompts, and personal records.

RepSetForge syncs app data through your private iCloud database using Apple's CloudKit service. The developer does not run a separate backend for v1.0.

## Apple Health Data

If you grant permission, RepSetForge reads heart rate, active energy burned, body weight, and body-fat measurements from Apple Health to show workout and body-trend context inside the app.

If you grant permission, RepSetForge writes completed strength workouts to Apple Health so they appear in Apple Health and Fitness.

RepSetForge remains functional if Apple Health permission is denied.

## Tracking, Sale, And Sharing

RepSetForge does not use third-party SDKs, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking. RepSetForge does not sell your data. RepSetForge does not share your Health data or app data with advertisers, data brokers, or third parties.

## Deleting Data

Settings > Delete All Data removes RepSetForge app records from the app database and requests deletion of Apple Health workouts that RepSetForge created. Data already managed by Apple Health or iCloud is also subject to Apple's account, iCloud, and Health data controls.
